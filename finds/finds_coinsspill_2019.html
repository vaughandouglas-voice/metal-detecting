Find: Coin Spill (2019)

Date January 2019
Location West Seattle, WA
Item Various US coins
Material 3 quarters, 10 dimes, 4 nickels, 2 cents
Value $1.97

A coin spill occurs when a concentration of coins is located in one spot — the metal detector continues to signal additional targets even after several coins have already been removed. This January 2019 find in West Seattle yielded 19 coins totaling $1.97.
