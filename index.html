About

Welcome to my metal detecting journey! I'm Vaughan Douglas, and I've been exploring the hidden treasures beneath our feet for years. From jewelry to coins, from everyday objects to surprising discoveries, every hunt brings new excitement.

This hobby has taken me across parks, beaches, and historical sites, connecting me with history and the thrill of discovery. Join me as I document my finds, share hunt logs, and showcase some creative projects made from my discoveries.
