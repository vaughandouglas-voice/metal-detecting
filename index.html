About

Welcome to my metal detecting journey. I'm Vaughan Douglas, and I've been exploring the hidden treasures beneath our feet for years. From jewelry to coins, from everyday objects to surprising discoveries, every hunt can bring new excitement.

This hobby has taken me across parks, beaches, and historical sites, connecting me with history and the thrill of discovery. Here, in this site, I document my finds, share hunt logs, and showcase some creative projects of other hobbies like wood burning.

The cover shot was taken in December 2018 at Edmonds, WA. The trusty Minelab XTerra 705 that found me some fun treasures despite being ill-treated. The coil eventually gave up after getting soaked - I hadn't realized the coil cover had split and allowed water in.
